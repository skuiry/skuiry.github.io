Support the IAHF!

The Italian American Heritage Foundation (IAHF) is a 501(c)(3) organization. Our passion is to bring a little bit of Italy to all the community. We offer college scholarships, Italian language and cooking classes, events, Italian-American library and historic photo archive, and we host the annual Italian Family Festa. We need your time, talent and treasure to make this happen. Get involved now by:

  1. Share your talent! Are you social media savvy, have a graphic artist flair, have a knack for fundraising? Then let us know at iahfsj@iahfsj.org.

  2. Donate your treasure, any amount is greatly appreciate. Donate online (below) or mail a check to 425 N 4th St, San Jose, CA 95112, please make checks out to IAHF and write "donation" on the memo line. We'll give you our eternal thanks and a tax donation receipt.

  3. Join the IAHF family by becoming a member. Click/tap here for membership information.

Scholarships

Each year, the IAHF honors students with scholarships to to assist in their financial needs and help promote greater interest in Italian culture among the young people of Italian heritage.
