Events and Activities

The IAHF is committed to holding events and festivities that will make you feel as if you're in the heart of Italia again! Come join us at Festa, our Regional Lunches, and more! Whether you are by yourself or with your family, you will have an amazing experience!

  • Italian Family Festa

    Share the day with friends and family, savor authentic Italian foods, fantastic live entertainment, experience our cultural village, and stroll the many wonderful arts and crafts merchants. We have something for every age. Grab a lawn chair or a blanket and join us for a day of fun and festivities!

  • Annual Crab Feed

    Come down to the IAHf for an exquisite crab-filled dinner. We will be offering fresh crab (by the bucket!), salad, penne al ragu, garlic bread, and dessert! You can mingle with others and dance to DJ Mark Hottel before the dinner begins. It will be an unforgettable experience!

  • Interested in volunteering?

    Please contact Ken Borelli at kjosephb@aol.com if you would like to volunteer to work on any of these events. We want to extend a special welcome and invite our many new members to join us as we work on these and several more exciting events celebrating our to Italian American Heritage.
