Benvenuti! Welcome!

We are the IAHF, the Italian American Heritage Foundation!

What is the IAHF?

The Italian American Heritage Foundation offers many services to our members and our supporters. We own and manage the Italian American Heritage Foundation Cultural Center that features a research library, a history of Italians in Santa Clara County photo exhibition, several other exhibitions involving Italian heritage, a social hall with a complete kitchen, and several offices.

Who are you guys?

  • We are governed by a Board of Directors, which meets at the Cultural Center on the second Tuesday of each month. The Italian American Heritage Foundation is one of the largest and oldest Italian Cultural Centers on the West Coast. We have a monthly newsletter, which is sent to members. It includes information on upcoming events, foreign and local articles on anything Italian, and historical information on Italian Americans in Santa Clara County.

The IAHF is fighting COVID-19!

The IAHF shares the concern regarding the world-wide outbreak of the Covid-19 virus. Rest assured that the situation is being closely monitored and the IAHF will be taking actions as recommended by the Santa Clara County Health Department as outlined on www.sccgov.org/coronavirus. To this effect, we have already procured gloves and additional disinfectants for use at the hall, cancelled our movie premiere event, St. Joseph’s Table, and have now deferred the Crab Feed to an unknown date; and will post any further changes, cancellations or deferments on our website. Our primary concern is for the safety and well-being of our members and community.

Events and Activities

The IAHF is committed to holding events and festivities that will make you feel as if you're in the heart of Italia again! Come join us at Festa, our Regional Lunches, and more! Whether you are by yourself or with your family, you will have an amazing experience!

  • Italian Family Festa

    Share the day with friends and family, savor authentic Italian foods, fantastic live entertainment, experience our cultural village, and stroll the many wonderful arts and crafts merchants. We have something for every age. Grab a lawn chair or a blanket and join us for a day of fun and festivities!

  • Annual Crab Feed

    Come down to the IAHf for an exquisite crab-filled dinner. We will be offering fresh crab (by the bucket!), salad, penne al ragu, garlic bread, and dessert! You can mingle with others and dance to DJ Mark Hottel before the dinner begins. It will be an unforgettable experience!

  • Interested in volunteering?

    Please contact Ken Borelli at kjosephb@aol.com if you would like to volunteer to work on any of these events. We want to extend a special welcome and invite our many new members to join us as we work on these and several more exciting events celebrating our to Italian American Heritage.

IAHF Membership

Become a member!

Become an IAHF member and help us preserve, promote and celebrate Italian and Italian-American heritage and culture. Our members enjoy reduced event costs, access to our dual language library, a monthly newsletter, and many other excellent benefits. More importantly, they enjoy networking with others that share the same passion and love of Italy as you do. Like the sound of our association? Want to become a member and join us? We look forward to welcoming you as our latest member! Being Italian is optional! For more information email us at membership@iahfsj.org.
