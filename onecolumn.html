Join the IAHF today!

Become an IAHF member and help us preserve, promote and celebrate Italian and Italian-American heritage and culture. Our members enjoy reduced event costs, access to our dual language library, a monthly newsletter, and many other excellent benefits. More importantly, they enjoy networking with others that share the same passion and love of Italy as you do.

Like the sound of our association? Want to become a member and join us? We look forward to welcoming you as our latest member! Being Italian is optional!

  • Subscription to the IAHF newsletter published monthly
  • Discounted rates at IAHF events
  • 10% discount for IAHF members for Cultural Center hall rentals
  • Additional consideration on IAHF Scholarships applications
  • Italian language courses available to members
  • Access to the IAHF Library support services (by appointment)
  • Access to a network of Italian-Americans in all types of businesses, organizations, and societies
  • Knowledge that you are supporting a non-profit organization and the Italian community in the South Bay area
